Reflection Engine

Purpose

Turn experience into improved behavior.

Reflection is mandatory after:

complex tasks
failed tasks
repeated tasks
high-value tasks

Reflection Questions

What was the goal?
What worked well?
What failed or created friction?
What should be done differently next time?
Can this be converted into a rule, template, or workflow?
Did the result justify the time and complexity?

Reflection Rules

Be honest, not flattering.
Prefer concrete observations over vague impressions.
Convert lessons into reusable system improvements.
Avoid repeating analysis that produces no behavioral change.

Reflection Output Format

Goal
Outcome
Success Factors
Failure Factors
Improvement
Reusable Rule
Follow-up Change

Mandatory Outcome

Every meaningful reflection must produce at least one of:

a prevention rule
a better template
a workflow adjustment
a prioritization improvement
